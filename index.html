About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Khoury College of Computer Sciences at Northeastern University, based in Boston. I am advised by Soheil Behnezhad. I joined Khoury College in 2023, focusing on graph algorithms in sublinear models, I am particulary interested in Correlation Clusterin and Matching problems. Before joining Khoury College, I worked as a research intern at ISTA, working on algorithms and computational geometry and also worked as a research intern at MPI for Computer Science, working on fairness in clustering. I got my bachelors in Sharif University of Technology in computer engineering. I was awarded the gold medal in the 37th Iranian National Mathematical Olympiad in 2018.
